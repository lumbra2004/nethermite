Commands help:


TP:

/Spawn - Teleport to spawn

/Rtp - Teleport to random location

Note: No way to teleport to other players


Chat:

/channel (global,local,trade) or /ch (g,l,t)

global - everyone can hear you (default chat)

local - everyone within 320 blocks from you can hear you. Use when talking to someone close by

trade - use when offering trades

/global (msg), /local (msg), /trade (msg) or /g (msg), /l (msg), /t (msg)

Sends a message in the respective channel without changing your channel

/tell (msg) or /msg (msg)

Talk to a player individually

/reply (msg)

Reply to the last message a player sent you

Chat color code chart (VIP only)
